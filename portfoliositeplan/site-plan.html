The purpose of creating my portfolio website is to showcase my skills, experiences, and accomplishments in a professional and engaging manner. Content

Home page

The home page serves as an introduction, providing visitors with a glimpse of my portfolio, a concise overview of myself, and a list of my skills. Through the navigation bar, visitors can easily access additional options, ideas, and information. Its main purpose is to offer a brief overview of my work, with key content accessible via the navigation bar options: "My Work," "About Me," and "Contact." As I complete more advanced projects over time, the "My Work" section will highlight my top accomplishments, showcasing my greatest achievements in this introductory space.

My Work

Initially, the number of images for "My Work" will be limited, reflecting the relatively small number of completed projects. Contact

Each contact method will be represented by its corresponding icon or logo, facilitating easy recognition for visitors and fostering seamless communication. Clear and accessible links to each contact method will be available, ensuring straightforward redirection for visitors. Upon visiting the contact page, individuals will find a range of communication options tailored to their preferences. These diverse methods ensure that if one option doesn't suit a visitor, they have alternatives to choose from, enhancing their ability to connect with me effectively.

Text for About Me: "Passionate and driven individual embarking on a journey to master the intricacies of computer science. With an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I am continuously seeking opportunities to deepen my understanding and skills in this dynamic field. My Work

In contrast to the "My Work" section featured on the home page, this page will exhibit a comprehensive collection of past, ongoing, and upcoming projects that I wish to highlight. Each project will adhere to the same format as seen in the "My Work" section on the home page, featuring a project title, a brief description, and the technologies utilized in its development (such as HTML, CSS, Python, etc.). Due to the limited number of projects available, the wireframe will present only a select few. Currently, the sole project showcased will be Aqua Adrenaline, until additional projects are completed and integrated.

About Me

Text for About Me: "Hello! My name is Jason Beckstead, and I'm excited to share a bit about myself with you. I was born and raised in the beautiful state of Utah, where I spent my formative years exploring its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture. At the age of eighteen, I embarked on a transformative journey to London, England, where I dedicated two years to volunteer work. This experience broadened my horizons and instilled in me a deep appreciation for global perspectives and community service. Upon returning to Utah, I found my calling in the field of education as a paraprofessional, later transitioning into applied behavior analysis (ABA) therapy as a Registered Behavior Technician (RBT). Working closely with individuals with diverse needs, I discovered the profound impact of compassionate support and evidence-based interventions. As my passion for understanding human behavior and cognition grew, I decided to pursue higher education. I initially enrolled in an online program at BYU-Idaho with a focus on psychology. However, my journey took an unexpected turn when I realized my fascination with technology and problem-solving led me to embrace computer science. Outside of academics and work, you can often find me indulging in my hobbies. I have a deep love for art and enjoy expressing myself through drawing. Fitness is also a significant part of my life, and I regularly hit the gym to stay active and energized. When I'm not immersed in my studies or pursuing my passions, I unwind by diving into the world of video games. Above all, I cherish moments spent with my loved ones, whether it's sharing laughs, exploring new places, or simply enjoying each other's company. I am continually seeking opportunities for growth and learning, whether it's through formal education, hands-on experiences, or the wealth of knowledge available online. I am excited about the journey ahead and the chance to make meaningful contributions in both my professional and personal pursuits. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. I look forward to connecting with you further!"

Contact

The contact page will provide diverse channels for audience communication, such as email (becksteadjason2@gmail.com), phone (my phone number), WhatsApp (linked to my WhatsApp account), and LinkedIn (linked to my LinkedIn profile). Additionally, a direct message form for user inquiries will be available in the future. Each communication avenue will be represented by an image, and hovering over the image will reveal a clickable link, seamlessly directing visitors to their preferred method of contact.
